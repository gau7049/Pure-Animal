Feeding Animals

Feeding animals is crucial for their health, growth, and productivity. Proper nutrition supports their well-being, sustains agriculture, and helps maintain ecological balance. The right amount of balanced feed is essential for different species and purposes, ensuring their health and optimal performance.

Here Animals are called Pure, Due to their innocence and faithfulness, If you observe, once you give a meal to any animal, the next day at a similar time and place, they return with hope for another meal. This innocence indicates their capacity for feelings and thinking. It's heartwarming how such animals enrich human lives.That's why we refer to these animals as 'Pure Animals' because of their genuine feelings.
